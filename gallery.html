Visualizations

Research Gallery

CFD simulations, experimental results, and flow visualizations from research on sweeping jet film cooling, fluidic oscillators, turbine vane aerothermodynamics, turbulent combustion, and spray dynamics.

CFD + Exp
CFD Experimental Film Cooling

Development of Sweeping Jet Film Cooling

End-to-end development from flat plate baseline geometry to nozzle guide vane implementation — comparing CFD predictions with experimental measurements of film effectiveness and heat transfer coefficient.

URANS
URANS Impingement

Sweeping Jet Impingement on a Curved Surface

Unsteady Reynolds-Averaged Navier–Stokes simulation of a sweeping jet impinging on a concave curved surface. Captures the oscillating stagnation zone and time-averaged Nusselt number distribution.

CFD · Unsteady
CFD Fluidic Oscillator

Unsteady Flowfield of an Oscillating Jet

Time-resolved CFD visualization of the internal and external flow of a fluidic oscillator, showing the bistable switching mechanism and self-sustained jet oscillation driven by internal feedback channels.

Turbine Vane
CFD Experimental Film Cooling

Sweeping Jet Film Cooling on a Turbine Vane

Film cooling effectiveness distribution on an OSU turbine nozzle guide vane using sweeping jet holes. Both CFD (RANS/URANS) and IR thermography experimental measurements are compared.

Experimental · IR
Experimental Film Effectiveness OSU Vane

Adiabatic Film Effectiveness on the Suction Side

Spanwise-averaged adiabatic film effectiveness on the suction side of the OSU nozzle guide vane. Measured via IR thermography at multiple blowing ratios (M = 0.5, 1.0, 1.5, 2.0).

CFD · Flow Field
CFD Fluidic Oscillator RANS

Fluidic Oscillator Flow Field (CFD)

Steady and unsteady RANS simulation of the internal flow field of a fluidic oscillator. Contours of velocity magnitude, pressure, and turbulent kinetic energy reveal the feedback loop mechanism driving jet oscillation.

CFD · Steady
CFD Impingement Heat Transfer

Steady Jet Impingement (CFD)

RANS simulation of steady confined jet impingement showing the stagnation point heat transfer, wall jet development, and Nusselt number distribution on the target surface. Validated against experimental Nusselt data.

Q-criterion · Mach
CFD Q-criterion Mach / Vorticity

Iso-surface of Q-criterion colored by Mach Number and Vorticity

Three-dimensional iso-surface of the Q-criterion identifying coherent vortical structures in the jet wake. Color-mapped by Mach number and vorticity magnitude to highlight compressibility and rotational flow regions.

URANS · Impingement
CFD URANS Impingement

Sweeping Jet Impingement (CFD)

Unsteady simulation of sweeping jet impingement showing the time-resolved oscillation of the impinging stagnation region on a flat target surface. Time-averaged heat transfer is compared with steady jet baseline.

Q-criterion · Nusselt
CFD Q-criterion Nusselt Number

Iso-surface of Q-criterion colored by Mach Number and Surface Nusselt Number

Combined visualization: vortical iso-surface colored by local Mach number with the target wall surface colored by Nusselt number, linking the vortex dynamics to the local heat transfer augmentation.

CFD · Film Cooling
CFD Film Cooling Sweeping Jet

Sweeping Jet Film Cooling (CFD)

Three-dimensional RANS/URANS simulation showing coolant jet trajectories and temperature contours for sweeping jet holes on a flat plate model. Lateral spreading of the oscillating coolant plume is clearly resolved.

Experimental · IR
Experimental Film Cooling IR Thermography

Sweeping Jet Film Cooling (Experimental)

Infrared thermography measurements of the adiabatic film effectiveness for sweeping jet film cooling holes on a flat plate. Lateral spreading compared to baseline cylindrical holes at matched blowing ratios.

CFD · Crossflow
CFD Crossflow Sweeping Jet

Sweeping Jet in a Crossflow (CFD)

RANS simulation of a sweeping jet injected into an external crossflow boundary layer, showing the jet trajectory, counter-rotating vortex pair, and wake structure as a function of blowing ratio.

LES · Re 95500
LES Turbulent Jet Re = 95500

Turbulent Jet (LES), Re = 95500

Large Eddy Simulation of a high-Reynolds-number turbulent free jet at Re = 95,500. Instantaneous vorticity and velocity fields capture the shear layer roll-up, turbulent transition, and far-field self-similar behavior.

Schlieren · Exp
Experimental Schlieren Fluidic Oscillator

Schlieren Imaging of Fluidic Oscillator (Experimental)

High-speed Schlieren photography capturing the density gradient field in the external flow of a fluidic oscillator. Oscillation frequency, amplitude, and shock structure are clearly visible at higher Mach conditions.

CFD · Roughness
CFD Roughness Fluidic Oscillator

Roughness Effect on Fluidic Oscillator Flow Field (CFD)

Parametric CFD study of surface roughness effects on the internal and external flow field of a fluidic oscillator. Roughness-induced turbulence modifies the switching frequency and the spreading angle of the oscillating jet.

Experimental · Combustion
Experimental Combustion Optical Diagnostics

Optically Accessible Turbulent Combustor (Experimental)

Optical diagnostic measurements (OH-PLIF, chemiluminescence) in an optically accessible turbulent combustor. Flame structure, heat release distribution, and turbulence–chemistry interaction are captured for model validation.
